About Cue The Line


    
Cue The Line is a small daily game for people who love movie dialogue.


    

      Everyone gets the same five quotes each day. Try to answer all five as best as you can. You’ll get a score at the end — and you can share it to challenge others.
      Answers are revealed the next day.
    


    

      Intentionally lightweight — no accounts, no leaderboards, no personal tracking.
      Just movies.
